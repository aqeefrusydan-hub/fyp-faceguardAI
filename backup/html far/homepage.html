Why Faceguard AI?

Deepfakes can create realistic fake images. While useful in art or education, they bring risks like fraud and identity theft.

Conventional tools often fail to detect them. With deepfake apps easily accessible, privacy and trust are at stake.

Our AI-powered detection helps protect individuals, organizations, and media from deepfake attacks.
