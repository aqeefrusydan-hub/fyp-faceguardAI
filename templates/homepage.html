Project Overview

What is Deepfake?

Deepfakes use AI to manipulate images or videos into realistic yet fake content. While creative, they pose serious risks like identity theft, misinformation, and fraud.

Why FaceGuard AI?

Deepfake tools are easily accessible, and traditional detection methods struggle to keep up. FaceGuard AI uses advanced CNN-based models for reliable detection.

Our system helps protect individuals, organizations, and digital platforms from deepfake threats.
